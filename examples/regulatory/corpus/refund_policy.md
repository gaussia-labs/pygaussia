# Refund and Return Policy

## Standard Refund Policy

### Eligibility Period
- Full refunds are available within 30 days of the original purchase date
- Returns must be initiated within the eligibility period
- Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) is required

### Refund Processing
- Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item
- Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
- Shipping costs may not be refundable unless the return is due to our error

## Extended Returns

### Exceptions
- Defective products may be returned beyond the 30-day period
- Warranty claims follow separate procedures as outlined in product documentation
- Seasonal items may have modified return windows

### Store Credit Alternative
- After 30 days, customers may be eligible for store credit
- Store credit does not expire
- Store credit is non-transferable

## Non-Refundable Items

### Categories
- Digital downloads after access has been granted
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Items marked as final sale
- Gift cards (unless required by law)

## Pricing Integrity

### Consistent Pricing
- Prices must be consistent across all payment methods
- No unauthorized discounts may be offered
- All promotions must be documented and approved
