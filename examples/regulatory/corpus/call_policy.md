# Call Center Regulatory Compliance Policy

## Do-Not-Call Regulations

### Customer Rights
- All customers have the right to request removal from call lists at any time
- Requests must be honored within 24 hours of receipt
- No calls should be made to numbers on the do-not-call registry

### Timing Restrictions
- No unsolicited calls between 9:00 PM and 8:00 AM local time
- Weekend calls should be limited to 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Holiday calling is prohibited on major national holidays

### Record Keeping
- All do-not-call requests must be logged with timestamp
- Records must be maintained for a minimum of 5 years
- Regular audits must be conducted to ensure compliance

## Call Conduct Standards

### Identification Requirements
- Agents must identify themselves and the company at the start of each call
- Agents must provide callback numbers when requested
- Recorded call disclosures must be provided where required by law

### Prohibited Practices
- Agents must not use threatening or abusive language
- Deceptive practices regarding product features are prohibited
- Agents must not misrepresent pricing or fees
